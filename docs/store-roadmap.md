# Analite Store Feature Roadmap

This roadmap turns your idea into production-like store behavior in clear phases.

## Phase 1: Real Browsing Experience (implemented)
- Advanced product catalog with search, category filters, and sort modes.
- Rich product details with purchase context, deliverables, support info, and related templates.
- Better product discovery flow from listing to detail to preview/checkout.

## Phase 2: Real Buyer Journey (in progress)
- Persistent cart (local storage first, DB-backed later). [done]
- Coupon input with pricing breakdown and tax field placeholders. [done]
- Checkout stepper: Details -> Payment -> Success. [done]
- Shared cart checkout for multi-item order. [next]

## Phase 3: Store Trust and Retention (implemented)
- Reviews list with aggregate score cards. [done]
- Saved wishlist and recently viewed templates. [done]
- Vendor profile pages and vendor storefront filters. [done]

## Phase 4: Operations and Growth (started)
- Order history filters (date, status, amount). [in progress]
- Customer support center (FAQ, contact, issue type). [in progress]
- Basic analytics cards for conversion and revenue trends. [in progress]

## How we build it step by step
1. Implement one phase.
2. Run build/dev and test.
3. Collect your feedback.
4. Move to the next phase.
